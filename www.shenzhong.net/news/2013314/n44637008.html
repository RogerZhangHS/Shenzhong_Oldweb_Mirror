IC to me

深圳中学 2013-03-14

　　IC to me is the shoulder of giant. She helps me look further, observe deeper and better integrate with the world.　　　　　----Marina

　　IC to me is a kind of specific platform which supports me all the time and thus I can reach the vertices of life based on its help in the future.　　　　　-----nobody

　　IC to me is a spectacular symbol which symbolizes the significance of my dreams and efforts. I am not seeking for basking in the glory, though I have been looking for the proof which illustrates the rising to the threshold of success.　　　　　 ---- Chen Pengxuan

　　IC to me is my source of pleasure; IC to me is also the cradle of my dream. I believe IC can give my dream a pair of wings, to let me fly higher and farther freely.  　　　　　----Yu Wuyue

　　IC to me is like sunlight to little grass. She offers me so many choices and opportunities that I can grow up properly. Everyone in IC has the same objective and we are working hard together to achieve our mutual goal. It’s very enjoyable. IC is the most special curriculum in Shenzhen Middle School.　　　　　 ----Huang Qian
        
　　IC to me is a place full of friends, teachers and interesting people. It is a big party hall for all the people who enjoy the party. We chat with others, talking about our studies and plans, conversing on our dreams and future. Just taking part in it makes me feel glad.

　　IC to me is a sitting room in a cozy house. It’s a good place for exchanging ideas. We read books, listen to music and explore our sights with visitors who come from everywhere. I sit in a corner, listen to others and chip in every now and then.　　　　　 ----Shelley

　　IC to me is a lovely family. Cherishing the same dream, we endeavor to pursue our ambitions, ignoring the holes and boulders lying ahead. IC to me is a spectacular stage. Everyone can find a suitable position and add a variety of colors. IC to me is a heaven for hard-working students but a hell for lazy guys. You can lead an exhilarating and hectic life. If you are subdued by laziness, however, you’ll find life in IC is tough. We are full of passion and ingenuity. I believe we together will build a road straight towards our brilliant future! 　　　　　 ----Candy

　　IC to me is a place full of dreams. I can find what I can do, what I should do and what I wish to do here. I’m sure I can make my dream come true. I can find confidence, enthusiasm, persistence, braveness and optimism in IC. IC is also a warm place. One can be helped by any IC student. Whoever you are, you will be one of our family members.　　　　　----Hu Xin

　　IC to me is undoubtedly special. Here, we have a strong and powerful staff. My friends and classmates in IC are intelligent and diligent. Of course, we all have a lot of pressure. But, we are not, and will not be, pressed out because none of us is a study-only student. We are active and characteristic. To conclude, IC encourages me to become more extraordinary.　　　　　 ---Pan Sixuan

　　Blossoms are meant to be fragrant, for flowers will eventually come. These years of struggling are what pay the road for tomorrow. IC brings fertility to me. IC fosters me.　　　　　---  Liu Yalin

　　IC to me is a terrace that connects China and other countries. Here we can learn knowledge not only from our country but also from other cultures. All the subjects and information are conveying with the world. This gives us many opportunities in study and development. Besides, this can make a better atmosphere for us to go abroad and receive further education as well.　　　　　--- Liu Quan

　　IC to me is the powerful engine that impulses me to keep ahead on my way to the future. IC is just like a tenacious rope and all the students are joined together to make progress. Perhaps the way to triumph will not be smooth to advance; I would never surrender. I may be stressed out, but I will chill out, or I will be burnt out. Nothing much can be told. In a word, struggle!　　　　　---  Cloris

　　IC to me is the one dream that has already come true, and also the road that leads to the next dream.　　　　　--- Aurora

　　IC to me is a stage to show our talent. Everyone has his or her specialties, and the international curriculum offers us a chance to represent ourselves. Besides, IC is a bridge which connects the foreign cultures to us. It creates a brilliant circumstance of English-learning. In addition, IC is like an experienced guide in my eyes. It leads me to the gate of success and bright future. The most important thing is that all the people in IC are friendly and intelligent. They help each other, and make progress together.　　　　　--- Jenny

　　IC to me is another home, a place filled with happiness. There are people who have the same goal as me and we work together, laugh together; we compete, and help each other. It’s really a good and warm.　　　　　--- Imsey

　　IC to me is a warm family with a lot of friends to help and to receive help. We help each other because everyone has his or her own talent and everyone is born this way. And always there are the people who are better than you and who needs your help. You will never get to worry about study and life, because there’s always someone waiting to offer help.　　　　　--- Jerry

　　IC to me is the way I can attend the university that I’ve always been dreaming of. It’s my process. It’s self-improvement. It’s our family. It’s our start. It means precious opportunities. It’s hard work. It’s happiness. It’s my life. Ich liebe International Curriculum.　　　　　--- Nancy

　　IC to me is a set which consists of many active and hardworking people. People here all have their own dreams and aims while they are chasing them firmly.

　　 IC to me is a big family. People here greet each other with smile and care about others with warm hearts.　　　　　---  Fu Xuhan

　　 IC to me is a bridge, connecting my world to the outside.　　　　　--- Zhai Xiao

　　 IC to me is a window; with it I can see the beautiful outside world.　　　　　--- Steven Yang

　　IC to me is a little society. As we know, most students in IC have their own point of views and thoughts. Some students are really outgoing, while others are quiet. Some come to IC because they want to do something for their country or even for human beings. But maybe others just come because they want to make big money and get rich. Each of them has his or her own dreams and that makes IC a little society: different personalities, different aims, different lifestyles and different memories. After high school, the students in IC will go all around the world and make their own choices. To me, IC is a place that can help me learn how to keep in touch with different people in society. It teaches me that the world is not always the same and nothing is completely right or wrong. It also tells me what I’m like and how to be the one I want to.
--- Angel

I seek then I see all in IC------------nob
