IC 2013 – 2014 Curriculum Course Program Designations

深圳中学 2013-03-14

Language Arts (语言艺术学科)

1.ENG100H Honor’s English (荣誉英语) – Senior 1

2.ELASP100H Speech and Rhetoric (演讲与修辞) – Senior 1

3.ELAD200CP Extracurricular Debate (Elective) (课外辩论（选修）) – Senior 1 and Senior 2

4.ENG200AP AP English: Language and Composition (Admission by testing) AP® 英语：语言与写作 （需通过选拔考试 – Senior 2

5.ENG200CP College Prep English (大学预备英语) – Senior 2

6.ENG200CE College English: Language, Critical Reading and Composition (LCRC) (non AP) 英语：语言，阅读分析与写作（非AP） - Senior 2

7.ENGCR200CP College Research and Composition (Required non-AP) （大学调查及写作( 非AP)) - Senior 2

8.LIT300AP AP English: Literature and Composition (Admission by testing) AP® 英语：语言与写作 （需通过选拔考试) – Senior 3

9.SWL300CP Survey of World Literature (世界文学研究) – Senior 3

10.SAL300CP Survey of American Literature (美国文学研究) – Senior 3 or Senior 2

 

Math (数学):

11.CALCAB100AP AP® Calculus AB (AP® 微积分AB) – Senior 1

12.CALBC200AP AP® Calculus BC (AP® 微积分BC) – Senior 2

13.MATH300LA College Level Linear Algebra (大学程度线性代数) – Senior 3

14.STAT300AP AP® Statistics (AP® 统计学) – Senior 3 or Senior 2

 

Science (自然科学):

15.PHYB200AP AP® Physics B (AP® 物理 B) - Senior 2

16.COMS300AP AP® Computer Science (AP® 计算机科学) - Senior 3 or Senior 2

17.PHYEM300AP AP® Physics C: E & M (Grade 11: Admission by Evaluation) (AP® 物理C：电磁学 (高二学生需要通过选拔) - Senior 3 or Senior 2

18.PHYCM300AP AP® Physics C: Mechanics (Grade 11: Admission by Evaluation) (AP® 物理C：力学 (高二学生需要通过选)拔) - Senior 3 or Senior 2

19.CHEM200AP AP® Chemistry (AP® 化学) - Senior 2 or Senior 3

 

Humanities and Social Sciences 人文与社会学：

20.WH200AP AP® World History (AP® 世界史) - Senior 2

21.ECOMI200AP AP® Microeconomics (AP® 微观经济) - Senior 2

22.USH300AP AP® US History (AP® 美国历史)– Senior 3

23.EH300AP AP® European History  (AP® 欧洲历史)– Senior 3

24.ECOMA300AP AP® Macroeconomics (AP® 宏观经济)– Senior 3

25.PSY300AP AP® Psychology (AP® 心理学) - Senior 3 or Senior 2

26.HGEO300AP AP® Human Geography (AP® 人文地理) - Senior 3 or Senior 2

 

Arts (艺术):


27.ART2D300AP AP® Studio Art: 2-D (AP® 影音艺术：2-D)– Senior 3

28.ARTH300AP AP® Art History (AP® 艺术史) - Senior 3 or Senior 2

29.DRAM300CP Drama（戏剧）- Senior 3 or Senior 2
