梅沙书院全球招募外籍学术院长及优秀教师

深圳中学　2015-09-26　

梅沙书院全球招募外籍学术院长及优秀教师

Vanke Meisha Academy Recruiting Worldwide foreign school academic leader

 

Vanke Meisha Academy in Shenzhen is recruiting a foreign school academic leader and excellent teachers from around the globe.

Shenzhen is a young and vigorous Chinese city. With sunshine and beaches, openness, equality, competitive remuneration and freedom and we welcome you to our educational platform. 

 

Qualifications for The foreign school academic leader of Vanke Meisha Academy
1.Honest, strict and upright personality.
2. International perspective and recognition of multicultural ideas with great respect forChinese culture.
3. Clear objectives for education and a focus on creating a first-class school.
4. Value the effort and inputs of teachers.

5. Ability to organize a plan for developing the school.

6. Strong interpersonal, leadership and communication skills for building a multicultural teaching team.
7. Innovative ideas and openness to learning; capable of accepting and leading educational research and development.

 

 

Requirements:
1. 5+ years of experience as an academic leader; education management masters degree or equivalent required, doctorate preferred

2. Excellent physical health
3. Deep knowledge of international curricular systems, particularly the IB; 5+ years of experience as a course manager or counselor in an international high school; familiarity with the application for school qualification to the IB course system.
4. Expert in school management, including but not limited to:
a).  Course research and planning
b).  Teaching management and assessment
c).  Student learning assessment
d).  Teacher professional development
e).  Student academic development

 

We provide:

ü  Salary of 200,000 US dollars/year

ü  Apartment

ü  Social insurance and medical care

ü  Chinese official holidays

ü  Return flight to homeland once a year

Also needed:

ü  4 English teachers ;should have a teaching certificate and 3+ years of experience.

ü  1 AP teacher each of math, economics, history, psychology, physics, chemistry ，computer science, civilization ,art history and drama.

ü  1 University Guidance Counselor with 2+ years of experience in an American high school with good reputation.

 

Contact us

ü  Send your CV to vma@vanke.com

ü  We will arrange the time and method of the interview

ü  Contact number: 86-0755-66618500

 

About Vanke Meisha Academy in Shenzhen

ü  Vanke Meisha Academy in Shenzhen is an international school collaboration between Vanke，the largest property developer in the world and one of the top 10 high schools in China.

ü  Our motto is“A Successful Future through Creation”with the objective of cultivating world citizens and leaders with a Chinese spirit.

ü  The campus is located at Dameisha Beach Park on 180,000 square meters

ü  There will be 25 students in each class for a total of 1050 in grades 10 to 12.

ü  The academy is equipped with more than 50 classrooms and functional spaces, as well as sports facilities, such as a football field, basketball court, beach volleyball court, indoor and outdoor swimming pools, rock climbing facility, jogging track, sail boat training base, and more.

 

 

 

万科梅沙书院全球招募外籍学术院长

招聘要求：

1、为人正直，真诚，严谨。

2、具有国际视野，认同多元文化，理解并尊重中国文化。

3、具有教育理想，对于创办世界一流学校的目标充满认同。

4、认同万科梅沙书院的办学理念，并能在各个层面贯彻执行。

5、具有严谨的治学态度，能为书院的学术发展制定战略、规划和实施方案。

6、具有出色的领导力和沟通能力，能汇集、培养一支世界水准的多文化背景的教师团队。

7、具有创新意识和学习能力，可以迅速接受并引领教育教学研究与创新。

 

具体要求：

1、担任学术校长职位五年以上，教育管理硕士，博士优先。

2、 熟悉目前世界上的主要国际课程体系，尤其是IB课程体系，并在开设IB课程的高中担任5年以上的课程管理员（协调员）。

3、 身体健康

4、熟悉学校管理，包括但不限于

a)   课程研究与建设

b)   教学管理与评估

c)   学生学习与评价

d)   教师专业发展

e)   学生学术发展

 

我们还期待：

4名英语外籍教师：具有教师资格证，3年以上工作经验。

AP教师：数学、经济学、历史、心理学、物理学、化学、计算机科学、西方文明、艺术史、戏剧。

1名升学指导：2年以上在具有良好声誉的美国高中工作的经验。

 

万科梅沙书院，一直对卓越的您，张开双臂！期待和您一起用我们的专业，引领和帮助每一位有梦想的孩子。
