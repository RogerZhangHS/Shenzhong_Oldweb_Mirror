USAYPT Acoustic Interferometer Reporter

深圳中学 2013-02-25

                                                                                                                QianJianing    Shenzhen Middle School

Build a Michelson Interferometer that uses acoustic waves. Use your interferometer to measure the relative speed of sound of a pure, elemental gas placed in one arm of the interferometer.
Theory
                                       
Theory
If d=m λ, m=1,2,3……
Then the two beams will constructively interfere
If d=(1/2+m) λ , m=1,2,3……
Then the two beams will destructively interfere
 d is the path-length difference
                                                       
Details
1.If the movable mirror is moved a distance λ/2, one beam will travel an extra distance equal to λ
      
 2.We do not need to consider the half wave loss here, because each beam will be reflected twice. That is λ/2+λ/2= λ, which means the influence will be canceled out.
 
Apparatus
∙          A cross-like tube
∙          Plastic wrap
∙          A changed Michaelson interferometer
∙          An oscilloscope
∙          An ultrasonic source and acceptor
∙          Reflectors

An ultrasonic source and acceptor（39000~40000Hz）

System Errors
∙          1.Disturbing waves caused by the pipe.
∙          2. Standing wave
∙          3. The instability of the energy and frequency of the sound source
∙          4.The extent to which the plastic wrap is stretched (reflection & penetration ratio)
∙          5. The inaccuracy of oscilloscopes, especially the graphic one
Random Errors
∙          Temperature, Humidity
∙          Pressure
∙          Data Reading
Development
∙          1. not use tube to bound
∙          2. use ultrathin glass to replace the plastic wrap
